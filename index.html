Our philosophy


We aim to design the most extraordinary, original and unique pieces of jewelry.


01

Design

Each piece of jewelry comes from our very own minds. We design minimalist yet very sophisticated pieces of jewelry which are all real head-turners. You will wear them with pride and confidence knowing they enhance your naturel charism.

02

Develop

Once a product is designed, we craft it by hand using only best quality materials in order to guarantee its durability and resistance. You will wear your jewels with the assurance that they will last for many years.

03

Deploy

Even though we are based in Paris, we do ship worldwide. Get our last item to wear it at the next Fashion Week in London or your next girls-only party in L.A. Our jewels are meant to be worn for any occasion, casual as well as formal.

Why chose us

FOVEA's collections are well-designed, unique and stunning items.
A unique jewelry

Our collection is crafted using elytra which provide incredibly bright and shiny colors to your items. Matched with your favorite metal, these colors enhance your natural charism and will have every head turn behind you.

Planet friendly

Be ecological even with your jewelry! At FOVEA, we craft our products from fair-trade sourced and up-cycles materials. Elytra are a bi-product obtained from the Jewel Bettle after it has been used as a source of food, and never from bred populations of bettles!

Made with love

Every item that we craft here at FOVEA has been thought, hand-crafted, tested and packed for safe shipping. Should you encounter any issue with your purchase, we are easy to contact and will provide all the support needed.

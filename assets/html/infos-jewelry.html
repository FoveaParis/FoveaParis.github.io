  • Colors: copper, green or blue (as desired)
  • Material: gold-plated or silver-plated brass ear wire (as desired)

    • In order to preserve the natural brightness of your jewels you shouldn't bring them into contact with water and humidity or perfume. Perfume, beauty products, alcohol, chemical products, lemon and other acid substances, but also salted water and chlorine must be avoided. It's thus advised to put makeup and perfume before putting on your jewels.

    We recommend that you regularly clean your jewels. You can use a soft brush and lukewarm soapy water to eliminate the dirty deposits (salted water, chlorine, sun oil). Rince them with water and dry them with a soft fabric.

Production

How are FOVEA's products made?

At FOVEA, we are proud to say that each and every item is individually crafted by hand in our studio in the heart of Paris.
The elytra we use to craft our pieces of jewelry are up-cycled and imported from Asia through a fair-trade commerce.
Our metalware is purchased from high-quality fabricants either in Paris, United Kingdom or Spain. All our metals are hypoallergenic, certified without nickel or lead.



Orders

How do I buy one item from FOVEA's collections?

Our website currently does not provide direct purchases. We systematically will redirect to our Etsy page for any purchase.
We are planning on adding direct purchase feature to our website soon. Stay tuned!

Which details of my items can I choose?

At FOVEA, we believe that to be truly yours, an item has to be exactly like you desire it. For this reason, we will always offer you the possibility (as much as our stocks allow it...) to choose between gold or silver metals.
Also, as you surely will have noticed, the elytra we use show an incredible range of bright and shiny colors. The major shade is green, and shows hues of either red/brown/gold or blue. Don't hesitate to tell us which shades you would prefer!

Can my order be tailored?

We unfortunately do not systematically offer tailored/customed orders yet. We are planning on making this possible in the near future. Stay tuned!



Shipping and deliveries

Where do FOVEA ship?

We ship our items worldwide. Shipping costs are calculated based on (i) where the items need to be sent and (ii) the dimensions of the package. Some items (i.e. large necklace) need larger packaging to ensure their safe shipping.
For specific shipping inquiries please email hello@fovea.com.

When do FOVEA proceed their shippings?

Our standard shipping hours are Monday – Friday, 9 am – 5pm GMT+1.
Please allow up to 3 days for your order to ship as our items are mostly made to order.

Is my package tracked?

FOVEA opts for tracked shipments as much as possible. As soon as the item(s) are sent, we will provide you a tracking number. This information may take up to 12 hours after shipping to be available.
Once your order has shipped by FOVEA, status will be available from the chosen carrier. We are happy to help but we only have as much information as provided by carrier tracking.

Is FOVEA responsible for the shipment?

FOVEA will not be responsible for any items damaged or lost during transit. Any claim should be filed with the respective carrier.
FOVEA will not be responsible for customs and import tarifs.
